Isabella Rossi – Head Chef

Isabella Rossi brings the soul of Italian kitchens to ArtsofCooking. With over 12 years of global culinary experience, she leads the team with passion and precision, crafting dishes that blend authenticity with modern flair.

Sakura Tanaka – Pastry Chef

Sakura Tanaka is a dessert artist known for her minimalist yet exquisite pastry creations. Trained in Tokyo and Paris, she creates fusion desserts that balance sweetness with subtlety, making each bite unforgettable.

Amélie Dubois – Sous Chef

Amélie Dubois is a culinary curator with a deep love for fresh produce and balanced flavors. She designs seasonal menus and oversees ingredient selection, ensuring that every dish is a harmonious blend of taste and texture.
